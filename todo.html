Things To Do

Located near ancient and historical temples, ruins and landmarks that allows one to be surrounded with plenty of amazing things to do. Things like touring the pyramids of Giza and learn of its history, and its construction. Tour the Valley of the temples and see the seated lion with the head of a pharaoh, the spinx. you traverse the desert on either camel back ride or on 4 wheelers. Visit Museums like The Museum of Ancient Antiques, which houses the largest Egyptian artifacts in the world of over 12,000 items on display. Bellow we offer a list of activities that one could participate that may interest them and other various options.

Private Tour

Enjoy a private 8hr tour where you will visit h2 historical sites like the Sphinx, the pyramids of Giza, the Egyptian museum with its treasures, the oriental bazaar of Khan el Khalili. Cruise Dinner

Ages 10-80

Nile Sailing

Enjoy a one-hour sailing trip on the Nile River in Cairo. Sail on a private traditional Egyptian felucca boat in Cairo, safely and hassle-free.		 $15/person
Ages 8-70

Want a truly unforgettable evening of entertainment in Cairo? Then don't miss the world-famous Pyramids Sound and Light Show in Giza! You'll hear the story of Egypt as seen through the eyes of the Sphinx over the ages, and see the breathtaking site of the floodlit pyramids lit up under the stars.		 $52 ranges by size of group
